History

mission

To become a leading global IT enabled services provider functioning as a catalyst to assisting client businesses to become world class institutions.

vision

AT intends to simplify the work and business processes of its clients thus helping them to focus on their core business whiles becoming more operationally efficient and customer-oriented.

goal

Use our time to save our clients’ money. Provide world class efficiency to our clients.

 

AT also has a human resource with depth in operations management. The operational team is made up of individuals with varied backgrounds who bring their passion to bear on the solutions we provide.
This team is what makes us stand out!

AT has has a team of tried and tested IT professionals with world class expertise in database management and design, web design, sotware design, mobile application design, networking etc.

AT currently has a data operating staff strength of 24, who are very experienced with decades of individual work experience from ACS Ghana (a Xerox company) as well as Teletech, a global customer service management company.

 

Elike Banibensu

He is the Lead Partner of Abelway Technologies. He previously worked for Affiliated Computer Services (a Xerox company) where he became the Assistant Site Operations Manager. Due to his six sigma training he was also appointed the Strategic Quality Director for Ghana in the company where he started from as a manager.

Elike subsequently joined TeleTech Ghana in 2011 after 8 years with ACS-Xerox as he wanted to broaden his scope in the direction of customer service in the outsourcing industry. He rose up to the position of the Service Delivery Director of the Contact Center which served one of the topmost telecom companies in Ghana.

Elike subsequently decided to focus his full attention on Abelway Technologies, which he founded, to work towards his vision of building an efficient operations and customer service support company with global standards.

Abdul Aziz Ismaila

Abdul is a Senior Partner of Abelway Technologies. He worked both in operations as well as in operations support thus knowing all aspects of the BPO work requirements. He was responsible for as many as 400 Data Operators and managing the accounts of 3 US based clients as the Operations Manager for the AETNA Division in ACS Ghana (a Xerox company).

Abdul continued into facilities and securities management. He excelled in that position for 8 years.

Abdul Aziz Ismaila graduated from the University of Ghana with a First Class Honours and has a double Masters in Information Systems as well as Business Administration.

Abdul is a died in the wool business man. In his pre-university days he was already marketing all kinds of consumer products in Accra and he paid his own way through University through his own efforts in marketing.

Abdul brought more vibrancy into Abelway Technologies with his immense energy and is an indispensible member of the partnership.
